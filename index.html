A New Frontier for Physical & Virtual Spaces

LiteHaus is an innovative project transforming abandoned buildings into functional spaces in rural communities.

  • Supporting culture through art and music.
  • Innovating technology with collaborative spaces
  • Enhancing local business with tourism and resources.

We are an open source organization, with a holocratic governance structure. The physical space will be available to members and guests after the completion of Phase II which allows the building to function in a limited capacity. Online tools and resources will be made available as they are developed.
